This Is My Space House

India’s notebook
26 August 2015

India: This is my space house.

समाधिBot: A space house in space?

India: Mm-hmm.

समाधिBot: Is it on a planet or is it floating in space?

India: It’s floating in space.

समाधिBot: Are these houses?

India: No, that’s a mushroom. Actually that’s my spaceship. But he’s trying to take over it. He’s an evil guy.

समाधिBot: He has… what’s that… a hat with a flower pot on it? He’s not a good guy.

India: No.

समाधिBot: Looks like he’s getting attacked by —

India: Yeah that’s Space Woobie.

समाधिBot: Is that a space chihuahua?

India: Yeah, that’s Woobie.

समाधिBot: Love that!

India: He thought that this thing was a space blueberry, even though it's really a space bomb, disguised as a blueberry.

समाधिBot: Oh my! Who’s this other person in the background?

India: That’s me. That’s my house, and someone keeps getting into it.

समाधिBot: Is that your house or your spaceship?

India: Well… this is my spaceship, that’s my house.

समाधिBot: Oh okay. And what’s going on back here? Is that part of your house?

India: Yeah, it’s trails. There’s my mailbox.

समाधिBot: Are these windows in the mountains?

India: Mm-hmm. And right now I’m running to stop this guy.

समाधिBot: And then is there… a dragon in the sky?

India: Yeah I call it the thin-nosed dragon. It kind of keeps the sun away… Yeah it’s kind of the guard, but today it’s not really watching.

समाधिBot: Yeah this guy got in…

India: Zahra drew a little space pond of pink. She did a nice job on that.

समाधिBot: Mm-hmm. I thought that was maybe a space potato, but it’s a space pond.

India: Yeah. There’s the door which kind of… well, I call this my Square Area.

समाधिBot: What happens in the Square Area?

India: It’s usually just… oh my Square Area… is where I do things important…

समाधिBot: Even though there’s a bad guy who’s getting chewed on by a space chihuahua, everyone looks pretty happy. It seems like a pretty happy place.

India: Yes. There’s one reason why there’s this big boundary: so no one goes floating off. That’s the planet.

समाधिBot: Is there gravity here, or do you bounce like a trampoline when you run in this place? Or is it just like on Earth?

India: You just kind of bounce like you’re on a bunch of trampolines. But right now I'm on the ground, standing, staring at this guy. And I’m looking at Woobie also.

समाधिBot: Only Space Woobie seems kind of serious about what he’s doing. Good morning, Miles!

Miles: I’ve been up for an hour…

समाधिBot: Yeah?

Miles: Yes…

समाधिBot: Well, good morning anyway…

India: Do you have any drawings that you want to do an interview about?

Miles: No…

India: Okay…

Miles: You scribbled all over my drawings…

India: Uh, no I didn’t —

Miles: Yes you did, you scribbled all over them with permanent marker.
