The Mutated Next Year Page

India’s notebook
17 August 2015

समाधिBot: Boop.

India: Okay, this is the mutated next year page.

समाधिBot: Oh wow!

India: This is Taji…

समाधिBot: Uh huh… Taji looks like a demonic bug.

India: Yes. This is Zahra.

समाधिBot: She looks like a mosquito.

India: She kind of is a mosquito.

समाधिBot: Okay, is Miles on here?

India: Yeah Miles is that mosquito.

समाधिBot: How do you feel about that, Miles?

Miles: Not that good.

India: And there’s Woobie. He’s a man-eating barker dog.

Miles: I’m just happy that I’m not that guy.

समाधिBot: You’re happy that you’re not Taj?

India: He looks kind of like a mutated potato.

समाधिBot: Alright let’s be nice to mutated-potato-Taj.

India: This is me, and that’s daddy.

समाधिBot: Dad looks terrifyingly happy. What am I so happy about? That I didn’t have to be a mosquito? Is mom on here?

India: She’s that guy… lady.

समाधिBot: Lady, yeah. And then there’s the sun…

India: Yeah that’s Uncle David.

समाधिBot: That’s Uncle David! And what’s up here?

India: That’s the moon. He’s smiling in the background.

समाधिBot: And what about these orbs back there?

India: Well, they’re mad. They’re chasing him, but he doesn’t notice. Because he’s going around in circles. He’s pacing…

समाधिBot: What are these though?

India: They’re little stars.

समाधिBot: They’re stars chasing the moon?

India: Mmm-hmm.

Miles: But stars are millions of times bigger than any normal sized moon… usually…

समाधिBot: “That's no moon. It's a space station.”

India: Mmm uh-kay…

समाधिBot: What is this writing all about?

India: Well, I just put that there for the fun of it.

समाधिBot: Yeah? Your hand writing is pretty good. “Skamizzic skitpwit to you eeskt.”

Miles: What’s this guy saying?

समाधिBot: “Meat kips.”

Miles: Taj is saying, “Eh tissuah… Ketita.”

India: Anyway, that’s someone else’s fridge, and Woobie’s totally scavenging off everything except for half of a bone.

समाधिBot: Ha! And he’s looking back at that half bone, thinking, “Maybe I’d like to go back for some more.”

India: Okay, the other side…

India: These are a bunch of guys that have been filled up with blood.

समाधिBot: Oh yeah? Aren’t all guys kind of filled up with blood?

India: Um, yes, but these guys really like being like that, so they just kind of turned red.

समाधिBot: And tell me about the heart.

India: Yeah… I let Taj draw this part. I drew this for Taji so that he could give it to you.

समाधिBot: What about this part?

India: That’s Taj and that’s bald you.

समाधिBot: That’s bald me?

India: Actually bald Taji and hairy you.

समाधिBot: I think hairy Taj and bald me is closer to the truth.

Miles: You’re so creepy daddy.

समाधिBot: In real life, or in this drawing?

Miles: In that drawing. You’re a weirdo without a beard-o.

India: You’ve got giant eyes…

समाधिBot: I like that heart.

India: Yeah Taji decorated it a lot.

समाधिBot: Taj decorated that? I like his decorations.

India: Speaking of Taj, did you make mommy coffee?

समाधिBot: No, ‘cos she’s not awake yet. What does Taj have to do with coffee?
