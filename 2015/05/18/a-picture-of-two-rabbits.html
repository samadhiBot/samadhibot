A Picture of Two Rabbits

India’s notebook
18 May 2015

India: This is a picture of two rabbits. This guy doesn’t really like this guy because… and this guy doesn’t really like this guy because he’s kind of mean, and really naughty. But these are rabbits… they live together but they don’t really like each other. It’s kind of like Ren and Stimpy, they’re very fond of each other but they get mad at each other. They give each other spankings —

समाधिBot: They do?

India: Yeah if they do something wrong, like to the meals, or what they’re going to sit on, or to the furniture…

समाधिBot: I can think of a couple other people like that.

India: Mmm-hmm…

समाधिBot: How come they have four arms and two legs each?

India: ‘Cos Taji really wanted four arms, because he wanted them to have twice the smack in their arms.
