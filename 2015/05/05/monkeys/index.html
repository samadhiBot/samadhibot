The Page of Monkeys

India’s notebook
5 May 2015

India: This is called the Page of Monkeys.

समाधिBot: They’re monkeys? I thought they were bears. They’re monkeys?

India: Yeah they’re bear — monkeys.

समाधिBot: Page of Monkeys…

India: This one, what are those monkeys called? They can eat cake… what are they called? Those two-tongued things with a forky tongue…

समाधिBot: Slow loris?

India: No…

समाधिBot: A forked tongued monkey?

India: Yeah. But… there’s such thing as them. This thing is an orangutan. That’s one of those long-fingered monkeys. That’s just a chimp. [she laughs] And that’s one of those tiny little white tailed monkeys, the fluffy ones.
