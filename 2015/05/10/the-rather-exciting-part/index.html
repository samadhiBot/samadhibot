Anyway, the fire’s so hot, and whenever they lick people, their saliva is a big blueish purple laser. It drools on them, so whenever they lick something, this happens: [makes squelching sounds].

But this thing is like a big large crest. It uses this for display and head bumping with combat with the other dragons or weird looking hamsters, or… But this thing, or this thing, or this thing, or this thing means it’s a male or a female. This one is a male, because the males always have a diamond above the cobra shapes.
