Valen’s Day Card

India’s notebook
14 May 2015

India: This is mommy’s Valen’s Day card that I worked on very hard, but a couple problems lingered around, like somebody tripping me, or tipping the table or wobbling my chair. But it turned out beautiful!

समाधिBot: It sure did.

India: Yeah. There was this color that looked like a brown but it turned out this color. This is crayon, but this I used oil pastel with it.

समाधिBot: They call that “mixed media.”

India: Yeah. I had to used a dark red and a little bit of dark purple to make this color.

समाधिBot: Is it any specific kind of butterfly?

India: Yes… it’s a kind of swallowtail butterfly. I’ve been working on it for weeks.

India: And this is the back…

समाधिBot: Tell me about the back.

India: The back was kind of made by crayon… a little bit of oil pastel, and some nice writing. This is only so she knows that it’s from me. And I drew this flower on the back, and this cat.

समाधिBot: Very nice. And a sun.

India: Yeah… a sun too. I kinda forgot to draw the grass but… it doesn’t really matter.

समाधिBot: Yeah I like it the way it is.

India: Let’s move on to the next one…
