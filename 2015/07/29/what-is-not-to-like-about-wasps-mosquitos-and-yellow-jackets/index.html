What is not to like about Wasps, Mosquitos and Yellow Jackets?

India’s notebook
29 July 2015

समाधिBot: What’s this one?

India: It’s about how much I do not like wasps, mosquitos and yellow jackets.

समाधिBot: What is not to like about wasps, mosquitos and yellow jackets?

India: Well, sometimes when you’re swimming and yellow jackets fall in the water, they sting you just to hold on, instead of grabbing on. And that’s what happened to me.

समाधिBot: You got stung in the water by a yellow jacket?

India: Yep. That’s why I don’t like yellow jackets.

समाधिBot: You know, I don’t think they do it to hold on. I think they do it just because they’re mean.

India: Yeah. The reason why I don’t like wasps is because one stung Miles. And when you’re minding your own business, sitting down or standing up, they just sting you for no reason.

समाधिBot: I got stung by a dead wasp when I was little. Can you imagine stinging someone after you’re already dead?

India: No… The reason why I don’t like mosquitos is because you’re always minding your business when you get bitten by one.

समाधिBot: Is that one half full or half empty?

India: Half full.

समाधिBot: Half full of blood?

India: Yeah, I could only draw the thing like that because… well you know.
