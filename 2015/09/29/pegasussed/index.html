India’s notebook
29 September 2015

[Break in tape]

Miles: You just ruined the whole interview!

Taj: We have enough time because daddy’s phone is at 100%.

समाधिBot: Okay we’re gonna do a do-over. Take two!

India: This is a picture of Zahra riding a unicorn.

Miles: No, unicorns have a unicorn hood.

India: Okay it’s a pegasus. It’s got a nice little beard… curly hair on its wing —

समाधिBot: That’s hair on its wing?

India: Yeah. Actually no, that’s just the wing part.

Taj: When are we going to get to the turtle?

India: Okay… this looks like a kind of tortoise. It has a hard shell and square shapes on its shell.

Taj: These are gooses, this is the sun, this is a flying pegasussed with Zahra on its back…

India: All horses… all pegasusses fly, Taji.

Taj: And a cat. And a turtle.

India: Turtles don’t fly.

समाधिBot: (The turtle is not in the drawing. The turtle is a toy that exists outside the drawing.)

India: Okay let’s get back to the interview. It has a nice little curly mouth.

[Break in tape]

India: Stop ruining the interviews, mommy.

समाधिBot: Will you tell us about the pegasus’ fancy wings?

India: It has nice curly patterns on its wings. Zahra wanted a flower right here, and a little curl right here. And there’s this nice patch of white.

Miles: Why does everything have reptilian eyes? 

India: I don’t know, that’s just the new trendy style. Next time they’re gonna have ‘X’ eyes.

Miles: What’s up with its mouth, why is it like a candy cane? Or a walking stick?

India: Because, Zahra wanted curly mouths.

समाधिBot: It’s smiling.

India: Yes. That’s another reason.

समाधिBot: Anything else?

India: Yes. This cat is opening its mouth, because it’s saying, “Rawwwrrr!” Because that’s what cats sound like when they’re screaming.

समाधिBot: The cat has been dropped by the hawk and is falling from the sky?

India: Yeah.

समाधिBot: It’ll land on its feet.

India: Yeah. I think so.

Miles: Why are the geese floating in midair?

India: No they’re not, they’re flapping.

Miles: Why are their legs on the ground, and they only have one wing?

India: Because I couldn’t make room for two?

Taj: Sure you could, you could just go like this… mmm-hmm… and make a wing right here.

India: Not on its neck, Taj!

Taj: Two hundred plus two hundred…

India: Shh, we’re not doing maths right now. Anyway, Zahra is not really in a good mood right now…

Zahra: Three hundred, fat head!
